The (Unofficial) Transformers Catalogue

Welcome to the Unofficial Transformers Catalogue! This page is dedicated to giving all fans and collectors a space to find out about all the new releases coming this year from the Generations line. We have everything from the flagship Studio Series and Age of the Primes lines to this years HasLab crowdfunding project! Feel free to browse our database to your hearts content.

  • Studio Series - The line dedicated to bringing the characters of the Transformers films and games to life in toy form.

  • Age of the Primes - 2025's new line and the successor to Transformers: Legacy, Age of the Primes is themed around the original Thirteen Primes and their descendants.

  • Exclusives - All of this years figures being released as exclusives through specific outlets.

  • Reissues and Rereleases - All the old figures being rereleased this year for those who missed out on them.

  • HasLab - Hasbro's premium crowdfunding oragnisation.
